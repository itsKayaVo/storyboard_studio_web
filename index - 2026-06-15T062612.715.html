Storyboard Studio
Draw panels, arrange timing in the animatic editor, add audio, and export a professional PDF storyboard or video.
Quick Start
1. Click New Panel to add a panel, then Draw to sketch (or import a PNG directly into a panel)
2. P Pen  E Eraser  T Text  G Fill  Z Undo
3. Open Animatic to arrange panels on the timeline and add audio
4. Click Generate Board to preview the printable storyboard, then Export PDF
